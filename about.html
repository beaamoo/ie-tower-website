About The IE Tower Snapshot

The IE Tower Snapshot website serves as a portal to the 180-meter architectural wonder of the IE Tower. Through this digital snapshot, visitors can explore the tower's 35 floors, each brimming with student hubs and advanced learning facilities. This virtual overview emphasizes how the IE Tower's spaces are ingeniously crafted to promote creativity, collaboration, and personal growth among its 6,000 undergraduate students.
